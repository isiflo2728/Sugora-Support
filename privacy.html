Privacy Policy

Effective: April 13, 2026

Overview

Sugora ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

Sugora does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device. Specifically:

  • Glucose readings are accessed via Apple HealthKit and displayed locally.
  • Meal logs, photos, and preferences are stored on-device only.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included in the app.
  • No account creation or login is required.

Apple HealthKit

Sugora reads glucose data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is used solely to display your glucose readings and calculate trends within the app. Sugora does not write data to HealthKit or share HealthKit data with any third party, in accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

Photos

If you choose to attach photos to meal entries, those images are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

Sugora does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services. There are no external network requests made by the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through the in-app settings. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Sugora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at minddose27@gmail.com.
